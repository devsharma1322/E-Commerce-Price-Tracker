# Trackazon — Amazon Price Tracker

## What Does This App Do?

Trackazon watches Amazon product prices for you — so you don't have to.

### How It Works (Simple Version)

1. **You paste an Amazon product link** into the app.
2. The app **checks the current price** and saves it.
3. On a schedule (cron), it **automatically re-checks every tracked product** for price changes.
4. If something interesting happens, it **sends you an email alert**.

### What Triggers an Email?

| Event | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Welcome** | You just started tracking a product — confirmation email. |
| **Lowest Price** | The product hit a new all-time low since you started tracking. |
| **Back in Stock** | A product that was unavailable is now available again. |
| **Big Discount** | The product is now 40%+ off its original price. |

### What You See on the Website

- **Homepage** — Shows trending/recently tracked products with images and prices.
- **Product Page** — Full details: current price, original price, price history, discount %, star rating, review count, and stock status.
- **Track Button** — Enter your email to subscribe to alerts for that product.

### Who Is This For?

- Shoppers waiting for a deal before buying.
- People who want to know when an out-of-stock item returns.
- Anyone who doesn't want to manually check Amazon every day.
